Track Your Emotional Journey Across the Web

MoodMark is a browser extension that passively tracks and visualizes your emotional state while browsing the web. It uses contextual data like browsing activity, typing behavior, and sentiment analysis to create an emotional map of your online experience.

About MoodMark

Understand how your online activities affect your emotional wellbeing. MoodMark helps you become more mindful of your digital experiences.

Emotional Awareness

Gain insights into how different websites and online activities affect your mood and emotional state.

Track Patterns

Identify patterns in your emotional responses to different types of content and online interactions.

Privacy-First

Your data stays on your device by default. No tracking, no external data sharing, fully transparent.

Key Features

MoodMark combines multiple data sources to create a comprehensive emotional profile of your browsing experience.

Sentiment Tracking

Analyze your text inputs (comments, messages, forms) for emotional sentiment to understand your reactions.

Typing Behavior Monitor

Track typing speed, pauses, and rhythm to infer mood states from your interaction patterns.

URL Context

Log visited URLs with mood snapshots to identify which websites affect your emotional state.

Mood Dashboard

Visualize your emotional trends over time, top emotional triggers, and mood patterns.

Local-first Storage

All data stored locally with export/import options for complete control over your information.

Manual Mood Tagging

Manually tag your mood at any time to supplement automatic detection and improve accuracy.

Installation Guide

Follow these steps to install the MoodMark browser extension on your Chrome browser.

1

Clone the Repository

Start by cloning the GitHub repository to your local machine.

git clone https://github.com/chirag127/MoodMark.git cd MoodMark
2

Open Chrome Extensions Page

Navigate to Chrome's extensions management page.

chrome://extensions/
3

Enable Developer Mode

Toggle on the "Developer mode" switch in the top-right corner of the extensions page.

4

Load the Extension

Click the "Load unpacked" button and select the extension folder from the cloned repository.

5

Verify Installation

The MoodMark extension should now appear in your extensions list and be ready to use.

How to Use

MoodMark works passively in the background, but you can also interact with it directly.

  1. Access the Dashboard: Click the MoodMark icon in your browser toolbar to open the dashboard.
  2. Browse Normally: MoodMark will passively track your emotional state as you browse the web.
  3. View Current Mood: Check the "Current Mood" tab to see your emotional state for the current website.
  4. Tag Manually: Use the mood selector in the "Current Mood" tab to manually tag your emotional state.
  5. Explore History: Visit the "History" tab to see your mood trends over time and by website.
  6. Adjust Settings: Customize tracking options and privacy settings in the "Settings" tab.
  7. Export/Import Data: Use the data management options in Settings to backup or transfer your mood data.
